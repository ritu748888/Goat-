About GOAT

GOAT is a dedicated box cricket booking platform designed for cricket lovers who want quick, easy, and reliable access to box cricket grounds. We focus on making box cricket more organized and accessible through digital technology.

Our platform allows players and teams to book box cricket slots, explore upcoming tournaments, and participate in competitive matches across different locations. With GOAT, users can manage their bookings without manual calls or ground visits.

GOAT also provides opportunities for brands and companies to advertise and sponsor box cricket tournaments, helping promote local sports while giving sponsors valuable visibility.

We believe box cricket is not just a game but a passion. Our goal is to support this growing sport by providing a simple, transparent, and user-friendly platform that benefits players, organizers, and sponsors alike.
